SIDeR‘19 Conference / 13th and 14th of September 2019

This year’s SIDeR’19 conference will take place at Muthesius Academy of Art and Design in Kiel for the first time in a German speaking country and absolutely close to Scandinavia!

We are so excited to be your hosts this year and to support the great idea behind the Students Interaction Design Research Conference. It is going to be a conference and a network party from students for students — our students are happy to welcome all of you!

At the same time the City of Kiel is celebrating its third “Digital Week Kiel” and with SIDeR’19.

We are a vivid part of it!

Important Dates

  • Sumission Deadline May 26th, 2019
  • Notice of acceptance July 21st, 2019
  • Camera Ready Deadline August 18th, 2019
  • Informal opening party: Evening of September 12th, 2019
  • Conference: September 13-14, 2019
